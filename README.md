### About Me

I'm a researcher specializing in **RAG systems** that extract knowledge from complex multimodal documents and generate optimized responses.

- **Ph.D. candidate** (coursework completed) in AI Convergence — Busan University of Foreign Studies
- **Big Data Researcher** — Institute for Migration and Multicultural Studies
- Designing a next-generation **Multimodal RAG framework** combining graph-based structuring with agentic reasoning
- Authoring an **XAI tutorial book** for undergraduate and early graduate students
